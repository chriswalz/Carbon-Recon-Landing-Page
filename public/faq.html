FAQ

What can you scan?

We scan Websites, Mobile Apps and API's. We use passive and active scanning. In the future, we may do code analysis as well.

How does ReconBuddy work?

Provide ReconBuddy a link to your website then initiate a scan. ReconBuddy will provide you with a list of vulnerabilities along with their risk assessments.

What's your pricing?

Basic scans are free of charge. Full Scans (Authenticated) are $199 per FQDN per scan

Who runs ReconBuddy?

ReconBuddy is run by two brothers with battle-hardened experience in Cyber Security and Software Engineering. They joined forces in their mission to secure the internet
